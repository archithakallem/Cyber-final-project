  • 🧾 File Hash

3. Advanced Scanner Modules

🌐 Nmap

Detects open ports and exposed services to measure surface exposure.

🛡 VirusTotal

Detects malicious signals and provides global threat intelligence.

🧾 WHOIS

Retrieves vital ownership, registrar, and registration data.

🔐 SSL Check

Validates digital certificates for secure connection compliance.

4. Scoring Engine & 5. Dashboards

📊 Scoring Engine

  • Exposure: Based on open ports
  • Threat: Malicious detections
  • Context: Based on configuration
  • Risk: Final combined severity

🎛️ Interactive Dashboards

  • Summary & Detailed Analysis
  • Visuals & Risk Maps
  • Target History Log
  • Actionable Recommendations
  • System Architecture Info

Visuals, Comparisons, & Tracking

  • 📉 6. Visualizations: Renders Bar, Pie, Donut, Line, Heatmaps, Sunburst, and Scatter charts dynamically.
  • ⚖️ 7. Multi-Target Comparison: Visually compare Risk, Exposure, Threat, and Open Ports side-by-side to identify the weakest link.
  • 💾 8. History Tracking: Backed by SQLite. Stores the target, timestamp, and scores to track longitudinal trend analysis over time.
  • 📧 9. Email Alert System: Fires alerts autonomously if Malicious Detections > 0, OR the Risk score exceeds 70.
